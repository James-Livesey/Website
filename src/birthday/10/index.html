Foreword

From James Livesey, founder and lead developer of LiveG

A decade ago today, LiveG was founded with the core idea to build software and hardware that makes a genuinely positive impact to our world. From the outset, we declared that it was our mission to bring that idea to life. As an organisation, we have undergone years of research into what makes technology beneficial to the user; and it was around three years ago since we began work on building our operating system, LiveG OS, based on findings from that research. More recently, we have settled on our current mission statement: to build open and inclusive software and hardware that's built to last. There is still a long path ahead of us to truly achieve that dream, but it is important that we are proud of how far we've come.
