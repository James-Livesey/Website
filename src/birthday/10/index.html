Our plans for the year ahead

Make LiveG OS truly a world-class mobile operating system

We're proud that LiveG OS is one of only a handful of operating systems that are designed for both desktop computers and smartphones. We hope to improve LiveG's mobile feature set by introducing cellular features, such as phone calling, text messaging and networking.

Expand LiveG Search to include more from around the web

LiveG Search has been proven to be capable of quickly finding information on the web with its unique search algorithm. We would like to scale up our efforts in crawling the web so that LiveG Search can become more useful for a broader range of topics and interests.

Develop tools to help our Contributors Community thrive

We are already working on building tools to help members of our community to make impactful contributions, including web-based user interfaces to help with translating our projects, and policies to reward our best contributors with the recognition that they deserve.

Have a go at our coding activity

Our birthday coding activity is back again for another year! Can you use the LiveG Astronaut framework to decorate this birthday cake?

View last year's celebrations

For LiveG's 9th birthday, we reviewed a year of intense development that saw added support for a variety of platforms.
