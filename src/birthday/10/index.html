The new Files app makes it faster to find files saved on your device. Files also includes a variety of sorting options that makes looking for a specific file based on its name, size or modification date easier.

Introducing Calculator, which we believe is the most advanced built-in calculator app of any operating system yet. Calculator has a variety of functions and features that are suitable for solving a wide range of mathematical problems, including those for trigonometry, calculus, probability, computational logic and more.

We're proud to be expanding our localisation efforts in LiveG OS thanks to our community of contributors. LiveG OS has now been translated into Catalan to expand upon our existing offering of English and French, and we're looking forward to introducing more languages to LiveG OS soon.

With the new Terminal app, LiveG OS now lets developers and system administrators easily run Linux commands. LiveG OS is now also compatible with many graphical Linux apps alongside our existing native support for web apps.

Readout Navigation — LiveG OS's new screen reader — makes it easier for users who are blind or visually impaired to use LiveG OS by navigating the system and apps through audio cues and spoken announcements.

For web developers, Web Investigator is our new tool in Sphere that can be used to debug web pages. Developers can evaluate JavaScript expressions and inspect the DOM to diagnose issues, find solutions and experiment with code.
